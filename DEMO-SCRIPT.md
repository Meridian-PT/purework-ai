# Pure Work AI -- 3-Minute Demo Script

## Setup

Before the demo:
1. Open the staging URL in your browser
2. Ensure demo data is seeded (POST `/api/seed` or run `seed.sql` against D1)
3. Have the landing page showing (`/landing.html`)
4. Optional: have a second browser tab ready, logged in as `admin@demo.com`

---

## The Script (3 Minutes)

### Opening -- The Landing Page (30 seconds)

> "This is Pure Work AI -- an AI-powered workforce operating system. One platform for everything your employees need: assessments, training, onboarding, compliance, and knowledge management."

- Scroll slowly through the landing page
- Pause at the Five Layers section: "Recruit, Manage, Perform, Learn, Protect -- every stage of the employee lifecycle"
- Point to the numbers: "8 assessments, 34 documents, 63 jurisdictions, 4 onboarding playbooks -- all built in"

### Login & Dashboard (30 seconds)

- Click "Sign In" to go to the login page
- Type `admin@demo.com` / `password123`
- Click Sign In

> "When your HR admin logs in, they see everything at a glance."

- Point to the 4 metric cards at the top:
  - "Assessments completed, policies acknowledged, onboarding progress, content documents available"
- Point to the team overview / activity feed:
  - "Real-time activity -- who completed what assessment, who signed which policy, who's moving through onboarding"

### Assessments (45 seconds)

- Click "Assessments" in the sidebar

> "Eight digital assessments built in -- DISC, Big Five personality, Culture Fit, Manager Readiness, Safety Competency, Sales Aptitude, Customer Service, and Workstyle."

- Click on one assessment (DISC is the most recognizable) to show the iframe modal
- Show a couple of questions, then close

> "Each assessment is interactive -- your employee answers questions, gets instant results with a radar chart and detailed analysis. Scores save automatically."

- Click "My Results" tab to show assessment history with scores and dates

> "HR sees the complete picture -- every assessment, every score, across the whole team."

### Content Library (30 seconds)

- Click "Content Library" in the sidebar

> "Your entire HR knowledge base in one place -- SOPs, workplace policies, job descriptions, training programs, onboarding playbooks, and compensation benchmarking tools."

- Click through 2-3 category tabs to show different document types
- Use the search bar to search for "safety" or "manufacturing"

> "Thirty-four documents ready to deploy. Searchable. Downloadable. Every access tracked."

### Onboarding (30 seconds)

- Click "Onboarding" in the sidebar

> "When you hire someone, assign them an industry-specific onboarding playbook -- manufacturing, professional services, tech, or general SMB."

- Show Lisa Thompson's onboarding progress (manufacturing, 45% complete)
- Click into it to show the interactive checklist with completed and pending items

> "Every item tracked. Every milestone signed off by a supervisor. You always know exactly where each new hire stands."

- Briefly show Marcus Williams at 15% for contrast

### Closing (15 seconds)

> "One platform. Every employee. Every process. And this is just the beginning -- AI-powered SOP search, automated coaching, email notifications, and payroll integration are on the roadmap."

- Pause
- Return to the landing page or dashboard

> "Any questions?"

## Key Talking Points (If Asked)

**"How long to implement?"**
> "Days, not months. We include 34 ready-to-use templates. Upload your existing docs, customize our templates, and your team is live."

**"What about compliance?"**
> "We cover 63 jurisdictions -- all 50 US states and 13 Canadian provinces. Every policy acknowledgment creates an audit trail with timestamps and IP addresses."

**"How is this different from BambooHR / Workday / etc.?"**
> "Traditional HR suites focus on storing employee data. Pure Work AI focuses on making your employees better -- assessments that develop them, onboarding that actually works, training they can access anytime. It's not an employee database. It's a workforce operating system."

**"What about our existing tools?"**
> "Pure Work AI consolidates what most companies piece together from 4-5 tools -- an HRIS for records, a separate LMS for training, a compliance tool, a document management system, and maybe an assessment vendor. One platform. One login. One bill."

**"Is this secure?"**
> "Built on Cloudflare's edge network -- the same infrastructure that protects 20% of the internet. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Role-based access control ensures employees only see what's relevant to them."

**"Pricing?"**
> "Let's set up a deeper conversation. We price based on company size and modules. Email sales@puretechnology.nyc."
