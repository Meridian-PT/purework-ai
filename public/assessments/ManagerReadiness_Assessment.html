Welcome to the Manager Readiness Assessment

This assessment evaluates readiness for a management role across 5 critical dimensions: Delegation, Feedback & Communication, Conflict Resolution, Coaching & Development, and Accountability & Decision-Making.

You will answer 20 questions using a 5-point scale from Strongly Disagree to Strongly Agree. The assessment takes approximately 8-10 minutes.
